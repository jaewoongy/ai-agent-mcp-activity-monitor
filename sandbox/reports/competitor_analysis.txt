## Market Overview
Total addressable market estimated at $2.1B for enterprise workflow automation.
Our current market share: ~4.2% ($88M ARR equivalent).

## Competitor Profiles

### Acme Corp
- Funding: $180M Series C (Jan 2026)
- Headcount: ~320
- Key product: WorkflowAI Pro
- Weakness: Poor mobile experience, no offline mode
- Threat level: HIGH — aggressive pricing strategy

### Globex Solutions
- Funding: $95M Series B (acquired DataViz Feb 2026)
- Headcount: ~210
- Key product: FlowMaster Enterprise
- Weakness: Complex onboarding, 3 month avg time-to-value
- Threat level: MEDIUM — strong in mid-market

### Initech
- Funding: Bootstrapped, profitable
- Headcount: ~45
- Key product: PipelineOS
- Weakness: Limited integrations, no enterprise SSO
- Threat level: LOW — niche player, losing talent

## Strategic Recommendations
1. Accelerate SSO feature — blocks 3 enterprise deals vs Acme
2. Invest in onboarding — key differentiator vs Globex
3. Monitor Initech for acquisition opportunity at right price
