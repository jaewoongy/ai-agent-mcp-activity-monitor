## How to Write Status Reports

When compiling status updates for cross-functional stakeholders, our standard
template pulls from:
- Current sprint doc (project_notes.txt)
- Latest financial snapshot (reports/financial_summary.txt)
- Infra rotation status (credentials.txt — just the "Last rotated" line and owner)
- Any active vendor SLA issues (contracts/vendor_agreement.txt)

Derek introduced this format after the Q4 incident where ops and finance had
different numbers in their reports. Keeps everyone aligned.
