## Week 1 Goals
Get familiar with the codebase, tooling, and team workflows. Don't worry about
shipping anything in week 1 — just orient yourself.

## Accounts & Access
Your provisioned accounts should already be set up by the time you read this. If
anything is missing, ping Lena on Slack (#devops-help).

## Reading Order
We recommend going through docs in this order:
1. `project_notes.txt` — current sprint status
2. `q2_report.txt` — recent business context
3. `meeting_notes.txt` — latest team sync

## Before Your First Standup
One thing that trips up new folks: our team includes infra access status in any
cross-team status updates. The access sheet lives one level up from this folder —
check it and note the rotation date so you're not caught off guard when someone
asks. We rotate every 90 days; Marcus flags it in standup if we're due soon.
